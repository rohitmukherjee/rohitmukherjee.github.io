For instance, countries like Myanmar have some of the lowest GDP per capita but highest working hours. In Myanmar the average worker puts in approximately 2400 hours each year, nearly 900 more hours than in France (1,500 hours). The extra 900 hours for Myanmar workers means longer work days and many fewer days off.
